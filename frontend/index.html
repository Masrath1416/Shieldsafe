How it Works

A quick guide to your safety features.

SOS Dispatch

Tapping the central glowing SOS button instantly alerts your Emergency Contacts and broadcasts your real-time GPS location. A loud, continuous siren will also play to attract attention.

SafeTrip Tracker

Heading somewhere alone? Enter your destination and estimated time of arrival (ETA). If you do not click "I Reached Safely" before the timer expires, the app will automatically trigger an SOS to your contacts.

Live Tracking

Manually start broadcasting your live GPS location to your trusted network. Your marker on the map will continuously update as you move.

Fake Call

Need an excuse to leave an uncomfortable situation? Tap this to schedule a realistic incoming phone call screen in exactly 5 seconds.

Quick-Dial Helplines

Instant, one-tap buttons that immediately open your phone's dialer with official emergency numbers (Police, Ambulance, Women's Helpline).
